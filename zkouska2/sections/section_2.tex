solve for zero, $f''(x) = 0$.

\begin{align}
    f''(x) &= \left( 3x^5 -10x^4 +10x^3 + 7x -9 \right)'' \\
    &= \left( 15x^4 - 40x^3 +30x^2 + 7 \right)' \\
    &= 1x^3 - 2x^2 + 1x \\
    0 &= 1x^3 - 2x^2 + 1x \\
    0 &= x(1x^2 - 2x + 1) \\
    x_0 &= 0 \\
    0 &= 1x^2 - 2x + 1 \\
    0 &= x^2 - 2x + 1 \\
    0 &= (x - 1)^2 \\
    x_{1} &= \frac{2 \pm \sqrt{4 - 4}}{2} = 1
\end{align}
